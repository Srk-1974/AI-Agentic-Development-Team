# App Demo: क्या हाल चाल? (Kya Haal Chaal?)

Here is the visual demonstration of how your app looks and works.

## 1. Chat Screen (The Messaging Flow)

**Action**: You open the app and tap on **"Ravi"**. 
**Result**: The Chat Screen opens.

*   ● You see the Premium Header with **Ravi's Name**.
*   ● You sent: *"नमस्ते! क्या हाल चाल है?"* (Green Bubble)
*   ● Ravi replied: *"सब ठीक है! मैं इस नए 'क्या हाल चाल' ऐप पर काम कर रहा हूँ।"* (White Bubble)

---

## 2. Video Call Screen (The Call Flow)

**Action**: You tap the **Video Icon** 📹 inside the chat. 
**Result**: The Video Call starts instantly.

*   ● **Main View**: You see Ravi in full screen (HD).
*   ● **Top Right**: You see your own face in a neat PIP (Selfie view).
*   ● **Controls**: Use the interactive toggles to **Mute** 🎙️ or **End Call** 📞.

---

## Conclusion

The code for these premium screens is ready in:
- **`App.jsx`** (Main Application Logic)
- **`App.css`** (Glassmorphism & Interaction Styles)

To run the live app, please execute `npm run dev` and experience the future of P2P messaging.
